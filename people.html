Andrew McShane
info_outline

Lead Director

"Tee pam paa. Butt cast. Jess's hair looks soooo amazing!"

closeAndrew
McShaneis the Director of Music at the Basilica of the Sacred Heart at the University of Notre Dame and Director of the Notre Dame Liturgical Choir. Andrew earned the Bachelor of Music degree in Church Music at Wayne State University in Detroit as a student of Ray Ferguson. At the University of Notre Dame he earned the Master of Music degree in Organ Performance and Literature as a student of Craig Cramer and Gail Walton. Andrew completed his education at the Northwestern University School of Music earning the Doctor of Music degree in Organ and Church Music.
Jonathan Hehn
info_outline

Assistant Director

"There were seven, seven constipated men in the Bible."

closeJonathan
Hehn(OSL) is a musician and liturgist currently serving as Assistant Director of the Liturgical Choir at the University of Notre Dame. He is a brother in the Order of Saint Luke and holds degrees in music (BM, DM) from the Florida State University and theology (MSM) from the University Notre Dame. Jonathan has studied organ with Michael Corzine and Craig Cramer. He also holds the Choirmaster certificate of the American Guild of Organists and has studied choral conducting with Kevin Fenton, Nancy Menk, and Andre Thomas. Additional time was spent at the Universit√§t Mozarteum in Salzburg, Austria. As a recitalist, Jonathan appears frequently both as a soloist and chamber musician, having performed in Austria and across the United States. He has won prizes in both regional and national competitions in organ performance. An active scholar as well, Jonathan has presented in the area of sacred music and liturgy and both national and international conferences, and his written work has appeared in The American Organist, The Hymn, These Days, Call to Worship, and other print and online periodicals. The Hymn Society awarded him a Lovelace Scholarship in 2011 and 2014, and the Emerging Scholars Award in 2012. He currently edits Sacramental Life, a quarterly magazine published by the Order of Saint Luke. Jonathan was the founder and long time director of the Tallahassee Sacred Music Conference. He is currently a candidate for rostered ministry in the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America. He resides in South Bend, Indiana with his wife, three children, and dog.

Alumni

The Liturgical Choir and its predecessor the Chapel Choir have served the Notre Dame community since 1973. Countless men and women have taken part in the Choir's ministry of enhancing prayer through song, using their voices to praise the Glory of God. As a choir, we sing together, bringing many voices into one full, melodic prayer; and while praying together, we have formed a family that spans over thirty-five years. The University maintains contact information for all Liturgical Choir alumni. To connect with fellow Liturgical Choir alumni, follow the following steps.

audiotrack Log onto Irish Online.

audiotrack Perform a search by clicking on "Directory Advanced Search" in the left-hand navigation bar. You can search for alumni by Contact Information, Employment Information, Family Information, Notre Dame Information, or Other Information.

audiotrack In order to search for all Liturgical Choir alumni, click on "Notre Dame Information" and then select "Music-Liturgical Choir member" from the drop-down menu next to "Student Activity."

audiotrack To narrow the search, add in other search terms such as first/last name or class year.

audiotrack Ensure that your Irish Online profile is up to date so that other alumni will be able to contact you!
