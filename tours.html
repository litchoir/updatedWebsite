Tours

In addition to singing in the Basilica of the Sacred Heart, the Liturgical Choir goes on tour once a year. This tour is international every other year; domestic tours are usually over winter breaks, and international tours are usually over spring breaks.

  • chevron_left
  • chevron_right


2020

location_onNorthern Italy & Slovenia

In the spring of 2020, we will be venturing to Northern Italy and Slovenia for our latest international tour! Destinations will include the cities of Padua, Venice, Vicenza, and more. Check back later for a detailed itinerary.



2019

location_on Florida

In January of 2019, we toured sunny Florida! Highlights included concerts in Tallahassee, Ocala, Orlando, Tampa, Clearwater, Fort Myers, Ave Maria, and Miami, a tour of Ave Maria University, two days on the beach, and a day at Magic Kingdom!



2018

location_on Spain

Our journey through the heart of Spain featured performances in Madrid, Toledo, Valencia, Montserrat, Barcelona. Notably, we performed a set in La Sagrada Familia, the largest cathedral in Europe.



2017

location_on California

In January of 2017, our California tour included stops in Los Angeles, Orange County, and San Francisco, featuring performances at many prominent cathedrals, as well as a trip to Disneyland!



2016

location_on Austria & Czech Republic

Our 2016 international tour featured performances at prominent cathedrals in Vienna and Salzburg, Austria, and Prague, Czech Republic.



2015

location_on Texas

Our journey through Texas involved stops in San Antonio, Austin, and Dallas. Over the week, we gave performaces at the Catholic Churches of St. Ignatius, St Rita, and Christ the King.



2014

location_onFrance

Our Choir's 2014 international tour included trips to Bayeux, Rouen, Lisieux, Solesmes, Chartres, and Paris - including a performance at Notre Dame Cathedral in Paris and La Madeleine.

Earlier Tours

The Choir has a rich history of domestic and international tours since the 1990s. Here are a few notable earlier tours:


  • location_onItaly, 2012: we traveled to the cities of Rome, Assisi, and Florence, and gave concerts for the Church of St. Ignatius, the City of Assisi, and a Papal Audience at St. Peter's Basilica.

  • location_onSpain, 2007: we performed in the cities of Barcelona, Madrid, Valencia, and on the Mediterranean island of Mallorca.

  • location_onFrance, 1999: our performances in France featured concerts in Paris, Chartres, Reims, and LeMans (the home of the Congregation of the Holy Cross, which founded and now operates the University of Notre Dame).
